Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly are "credits"?

expand_more

Credits represent computational units used by PressArk. Different tasks consume varying amounts based on token length and model complexity. 1M credits covers roughly 500-800 advanced agentic tasks.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

expand_more

Yes. Plans are billed monthly and can be adjusted at any point. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle, while upgrades are prorated immediately.

Do credits roll over?

expand_more

Monthly plan credits do not roll over. However, manually purchased "Extra Credit" packs never expire and are only used after your monthly allocation is exhausted.
