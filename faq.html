Product Basics

What is PressArk?

expand_more

PressArk is a specialized AI copilot integrated directly into your WordPress admin. It automates repetitive management tasks, content optimization, and technical troubleshooting using advanced Large Language Models with 116+ specialized tools.

Is this a WordPress plugin or web app?

expand_more

It is a native WordPress plugin. The primary interface lives inside your WordPress sidebar for seamless operational access. We provide a license management dashboard at pressark.ai, but the tool itself runs in wp-admin.

How do I install the plugin?

expand_more

Download the ZIP file from your account dashboard or GitHub. In your WordPress admin, navigate to Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin. After activation, enter your license key or configure BYOK to connect to the AI engine.

Credits & Billing

What are credits?

expand_more

Credits represent the compute power used for AI requests. Different tasks consume varying amounts based on token length and model complexity. A simple meta description generation uses fewer credits than a full site SEO audit.

Do credits roll over?

expand_more

Monthly plan credits do not roll over. However, manually purchased Extra Credit packs never expire and are only used after your monthly allocation is exhausted.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

expand_more

Yes. Plans are billed monthly and can be adjusted at any point. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle, while upgrades are prorated immediately.

Technical (BYOK)

What is BYOK?

expand_more

Bring Your Own Key lets you plug in your own API keys from OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Google Gemini, or OpenRouter. We charge zero overhead when using your own keys — credits are only consumed for our internal agentic workflow orchestration.

Which AI models are supported?

expand_more

PressArk supports GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet 4, DeepSeek V3, Gemini 2.5 Flash, and any model available via OpenRouter. Model selection is per-request configurable.

Is my data sent to third parties?

expand_more

When using BYOK, your prompts go directly to your chosen provider. When using managed credits, requests route through our token bank infrastructure. We never store conversation content beyond the active session.
